Cream Fetuccine Pasta

A creamy fettuccine-style pasta tossed in a light garlic–cream sauce with tender shrimp, thinly sliced mushrooms, and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Ingredients

  • 120–150 g dried fettuccine
  • 6–8 medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½–¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1–2 tbsp unsalted butter or olive oil
  • 2–3 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, finely chopped

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente; reserve a little cooking water before draining.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, melt the butter (or heat the oil), then sauté the garlic for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until softened and lightly browned.
  4. Add the shrimp to the pan and cook just until pink and opaque; season lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Lower the heat and pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently; let it simmer briefly to thicken slightly.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan until melted and smooth. If the sauce looks too thick, add a small splash of the reserved pasta water.
  7. Add the drained pasta to the pan and toss to coat evenly in the creamy sauce.
  8. Transfer to a plate or bowl and finish with chopped parsley and extra black pepper if desired.
